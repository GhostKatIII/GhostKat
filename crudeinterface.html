Enter GhostKat TV at Your Own Risk

GhostKat TV is an experimental streaming service that doesn't use a server! The media you view on GK is sent to you by other users viewing that media; while you receive the next moment of a video, you are sending out the pieces you have already seen. Your computer finds other users viewing the same file by using a magnet link. The link acts as a reference to the media being transmitted between users.

Our website only sends your computer the code it needs to find other users. After that, your computer is no longer communicating with GhostKat.org. We use a combination of WebTorrent, Ethereum, and Infura. These technologies act as the backbone that will replace traditional server architecture in the near future. Your computer uses Infura to ask the vast network of Ethereum computers for the magnet links. Anyone with an Ethereum Node can post to the GhostKat magnet link array (tutorial).

By replacing all mechanics of a server, GhostKat TV floats on a cloud of users and Ethereum. This website is an experiment in a truly unregulated internet. GhostKat TV has no ads, no sign-up, no plug-ins, no nothing. It's like we hardly exist.
